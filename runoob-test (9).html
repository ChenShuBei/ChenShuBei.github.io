Rooms

Hilton Hotel offers high-quality rooms for guests. Each room is equipped with comfortable beds, elegant furniture, and modern bathrooms. We are committed to providing guests with a home away from home feeling, making you feel comfortable and relaxed during your travels.

Dining

We have multiple restaurants and bars in the hotel, offering a variety of cuisine and drink options. Whether you want to taste local specialties or enjoy international cuisine, we can meet your needs. Our chefs are skilled at cooking all kinds of dishes, bringing you a delicious dining experience.

Fitness Center

Our hotel has an advanced fitness center with various sports equipment. You can work out here to keep yourself healthy and energetic. We also provide professional fitness coaches to provide personalized fitness guidance.

Meeting Rooms

We offer multiple spacious meeting rooms that can accommodate meetings and events of different sizes. The meeting rooms are equipped with advanced audiovisual equipment and conference tools to meet your business needs. Our professional team will ensure that your meetings run smoothly and provide first-class service.

Entertainment Facilities

Our hotel also provides various entertainment facilities, such as swimming pools, SPA centers, and children's playgrounds. You can relax and enjoy your time in these facilities. We are committed to bringing guests a unique leisure experience, allowing you to spend unforgettable time in the hotel.
